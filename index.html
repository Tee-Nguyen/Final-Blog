About Me
Hello! My name is Tina and I have a knack for fashion and coding. My hobbies include: traveling, baking, cooking, spending times with my loved ones and cars. I am not so amazing with front end work but I am trying my best. As you click on my portfolio, you will see that I've created a site where you can browse bags, specifically, Louis Vuitton handbags. This is the only project I've done so far, but, who knows? Maybe I'll add more throughout the summer. As an avid shopper, my favorite thing to look for while shopping on a website is simplicity. If it's too colorful, it's less appealing. If it's too complicated, it's not easy for everyone to navigate. I tried to make it as user friendly as possible for my browsing portfolio and hope to add more to it in the future!
